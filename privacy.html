Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-15

Inkblade Author Studio ("we", "us") operates this site and a Pinterest application used to publish marketing pins for our products. This policy explains what data we handle and why.

Data we collect

To post pins to Pinterest, our application uses an OAuth access token issued by Pinterest. We access only our own business account's boards and pins. We do not collect personal data from Pinterest users who view our pins.

How we use data

The Pinterest access token and board identifiers are used solely to publish product pins (captions, calendars, image packs, bundles) to our own boards. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

Data retention

The access token is stored securely and used only for publishing. We retain no user-generated content from third parties.

Third parties

We use Gumroad for product sales and Pinterest for marketing publication. Each has its own privacy policy governing data you provide directly to them.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our Gumroad store.
